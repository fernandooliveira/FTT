.panel

Sets up regions of the interface.

div.panel div.inspector div.content {content} div.head div|ul {content} div.foot div|ul {content}

.head and .foot are optional. On the face of it, this looks like it could be made much simpler - and it can, if you don't mind animating several properties when showing/hiding the .palette's .head or .foot. With this construct you can simply animate .palette top or bottom and the .head or .foot will slide nicely into view. Remember that animating multiple elements causes multiple browser refreshes (per frame). Also the height of the .head and .foot are flexible, so you can query them from your script before showing them.

.palette shoud be put inside something with overflow: hidden; else .head and .foot will always show.
